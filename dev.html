About FretMap

Fretmap is a free web application helping you master your guitar scales and shred with the best of 'em.

The app provides an interactive fretboard allowing you to pick your root note, your scale, and whether you're a righty or a lefty.

It's also responsive, meaning you can take Fretmap with you anywhere from your desktop to... where ever you can take your smartphone or tablet.

To report a bug or to make a suggestion, please send a tweet to @thathurtabit

Happy riffing!
